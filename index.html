Iman Iraei

I am a Master of Science student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Concordia University in Montréal, Canada, where I am advised by Prof. M. Omair Ahmad. My research interests include computer vision, object detection, autonomous driving, vision-language models, and efficient deep learning systems.

How can intelligent perception systems become more accurate, efficient, and reliable for real-world autonomous driving?

My current research focuses on real-time object detection and efficient neural architectures for autonomous vehicles. I am particularly interested in designing lightweight yet accurate perception models that improve robustness under challenging driving conditions such as small, distant, and occluded objects.
